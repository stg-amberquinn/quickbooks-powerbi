QuickBooks Power BI Reporting Integration

Application Description

This application is used to securely connect QuickBooks Online to Microsoft Power BI for business reporting, analytics, and operational insights. The application retrieves accounting and financial data authorized by the connected QuickBooks Online company and presents that information within internal reporting dashboards.

Data Usage

The application may access accounting-related data including customers, vendors, invoices, payments, bills, accounts, expenses, journal entries, and other financial records available through the QuickBooks Online API.

Data is accessed solely for reporting, analytics, and business intelligence purposes. The application does not modify accounting records unless explicitly configured to do so.

Privacy Policy

Information obtained through QuickBooks Online is used exclusively for authorized business reporting and analytics purposes.

Data is stored and processed within company-controlled systems, including Microsoft Power BI, Microsoft Azure, and other approved business platforms. Information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties except as required to provide reporting functionality or comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Appropriate administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards are maintained to protect the confidentiality and security of customer data.

Terms of Use / End User License Agreement

By authorizing this application, users grant permission for the application to access QuickBooks Online data necessary to provide reporting and analytics functionality.

The application is provided on an "as-is" basis without warranties of any kind. Users are responsible for ensuring they have the authority to authorize access to the connected QuickBooks Online company.

Access may be revoked at any time by disconnecting the application from QuickBooks Online.
